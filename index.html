Who We Are
ABOUT WSA

Western Skies Air is a Texas-based virtual charter airline operating within FSEconomy. We focus on realistic, immersive general aviation operations across the Southern Plains — cargo runs, passenger charters, and executive flights.

We value authentic flying: real weather, real procedures, real ATC communication, and aircraft that make sense for the region. Every flight logged here updates our live stats instantly.

Open to all skill levels. New pilots welcome — we'll get you familiar with FSEconomy and our ops culture quickly.
